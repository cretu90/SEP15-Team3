You can adjust your personal information by clicking the button "Edit". Your chosen password must fulfill the following conditions: It must contain at least a lower case letter, an upper case letter, a digit, a special character (Ü, Ä, Ö, ß are not allowed) and must be at least eight characters long. You can also update your avatar. The E-Mail must be a valid E-Mail address. To charge your account click on "Charge account". You will then be forwarded to the respective view. FOR COURSE INSTRUCTORS: You can sort the table of your courses by clicking on the header of each column. Click on a requested course to open a detailed view.
